ABOUT US

Arone Energy pioneers breakthrough technologies that transform lives by enabling access to green electricity for millions of homes in developing regions.

We manufacture low-cost, stand-alone solar grid systems and leveraging smart financing models to reduce upfront costs and increase electricity access. Our vision is to power every home with green electricity.

With this, we are driving the transition from fossil fuel powered generators to renewable energy in line with the Sustainable Development Goal 7 of ensuring access to affordable, reliable, sustainable, and modern energy.
